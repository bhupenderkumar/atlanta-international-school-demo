Our Mission & History

Atlanta International School has a rich history of providing an exceptional education to students from around the world. Founded in 2003, our school has grown to become a leading institution in the Atlanta community.

At AIS, we believe in a student-centered approach to learning. Our experienced teachers use a range of instructional strategies to engage students and foster a love of learning.

Our students are encouraged to participate in a wide range of extracurricular activities, from sports and music to drama and community service. These experiences help build character, develop teamwork skills, and foster a sense of community.
