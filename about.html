Our Mission & History

Atlanta International School has a rich history of providing exceptional education to students from over 60 nationalities. Our school was founded in 2001 by a group of visionary educators who shared a commitment to academic excellence and diversity.

At AIS, we believe that every child learns differently. Our teachers use a variety of teaching approaches to engage students and help them reach their full potential. We also offer a range of extracurricular activities that foster creativity, teamwork, and character development.

From preschool through 12th grade, our students participate in a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, drama, and community service. We encourage our students to explore their passions and interests outside of the classroom, which helps them develop into well-rounded, confident individuals.
